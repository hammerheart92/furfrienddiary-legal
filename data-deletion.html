How to Delete Your Data - FurFriendDiary

Important: FurFriendDiary stores all data locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers.

Method 1: Delete Data Within the App

  1. Open the FurFriendDiary app
  2. Go to Settings (tap the gear icon)
  3. Scroll down to Data Management section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion when prompted

This will permanently delete:

  • All pet profiles and information
  • Health records (vaccinations, medications, appointments, deworming)
  • Photos and documents
  • Weight tracking data
  • Activity logs (walks, feedings)
  • All settings and preferences

Method 2: Uninstall the App

Alternatively, uninstalling FurFriendDiary from your Android device will automatically delete all locally stored data.

  1. Go to your device's Settings
  2. Select Apps or Applications
  3. Find and tap FurFriendDiary
  4. Tap Uninstall

Data Deletion Confirmation

Once deleted through either method, your data cannot be recovered. We do not store backups of your data on any servers.
